The Contribution point break down, and How it equals real money.

User owned means that you have the means to actually use social media to make money! At least you can use our social media to make money, this is how that works.


Each user gets an account, that user gets hosting and other services included. On those personal accounts, you don't get to get points for uploading unless otherwise told by staff. For a bonus or a one time gift.


However you do get contribution points for things you do to the main SourceSocial.me site such as changing elements, adding to the site, creating features you think will help, and where you are told you will earn points.


Points then equal ownership shares of sourcesocial.me those ownership shares are just like shares of any other company, they have real value as we make profits by selling upgraded services such as dedicated hosting and much more.


When those profits are made they get split between the holding company that is the original investors, and those who oversee the management team (the business end). The Profits get split between the current shareholders (the Users that have Points when they equal Ownership shares). They do not get split based on the current shares available to everyone they only get split among the currently issued shares.


what this means is if SourceSocial.me makes $1,000,000 dollars for the quarter and we have 1,000 users that currently have 10 shares each, 51% goes to the holding company Petrucci LLC To take care of some things like reinvesting, and paying staff. the rest $490,000 dollars goes to the users based on the shares they own. so 10,000 shares are issued in this examples. So in this example, Each share would equal $49 dollars that means In this example each user would be getting $490 dollars for the quarter of the year.
